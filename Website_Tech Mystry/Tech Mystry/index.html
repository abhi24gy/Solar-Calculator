About Us

Our Solar Calculator is an online tool developed by undergraduate students at IIT Gandhinagar to help you quickly determine the potential savings that you can make when you go solar.
The solar calculator is one of its kind when it comes to pre-estimating the solar system sizing, solar savings potential, solar investment, return on investment and solar financing options of Indian power consumers from across residential category. Using this online solar calculator is extremely simple. All you need to do is input three basic details - your monthly electricity consumption, roof area, and budget. And the solar calculator will provide you with an unbiased snapshot of your solar potential and other details.
